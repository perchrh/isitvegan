Sources

Sources for the information presented in this app:
  1. Glossary of animal substances. The Vegan Society, Nov 2013.
  2. Which E-numbers and additives are from animal origin. Food-Info.net, Nov 2013. Referenced by the Vegan Society in (1).
  3. Current EU approved additives and their E Numbers. The European Union, Nov 2013.

Limitations

Only E-numbers and additives used in food are included in this app, in order to make it simpler to determine when food products are vegan or not.
